Spacewalking is an exciting and interactive event. The resort will provide each guest with a spacesuit, and each contestant will need to travel to the "Mars Space Station". The Mars Space Station has a defined track and a viewing venue. In the arena, participants can experience a sense of weightlessness in space and the intuitive feeling of touching the surface of Mars. After completing multiple levels, the player who first reaches the destination wins.

Location: Mars Space Station(level4)

Date: 8 pm every Wednesday

Requirement: 18 above(Please bring your ID)

Maximum : 20

According to the latest findings of scientists, there is much underlying ice on Mars. In the ice rink on the B1 of the Mars Intelligence Club, visitors can enjoy the passion of ice hockey and the incredible scenery of glacier sculptures all around rink.

Reminder: the temperature of underground glaciers is low, please pay attention to the cold and keep warm.

Location: Ice-world (level B1)

Date: 7:00 pm every Tuesday/Friday

Requirement: 18 above for participants

Maximum : 15 （ice hockey)
